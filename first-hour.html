The First Hour

Understanding the journey to The Second Hour

From First to Second Hour

The theme "The Second Hour" builds upon the foundation of the first.

The First Hour represents the initial phase of any meaningful endeavor—the moment of preparation, learning, and laying the groundwork. At ANTRAAL MUN, we recognize that every delegate's journey begins with that crucial first hour of understanding the rules of procedure, researching their country's stance, and building the confidence to speak.

The Second Hour is when that preparation transforms into action. It is the pivotal moment when ideas are voiced, alliances are formed, and resolutions take shape. Our conference is designed to guide delegates from the first hour of preparation to the second hour of impactful debate and diplomacy.

What to Expect

Whether you are a first-time delegate or a seasoned MUN veteran, ANTRAAL MUN provides a platform where your first hour of preparation meets your second hour of excellence. Join us to experience the transition from preparation to performance.
