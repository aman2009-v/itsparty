What is ANTRAAL MUN?

A platform for young minds to debate, deliberate, and shape the future.

ANTRAAL Model United Nations is a premier conference that brings together passionate delegates from across the nation to engage in diplomatic discourse. Rooted in the spirit of the United Nations, we provide a platform where students simulate real-world international relations, negotiate resolutions, and develop critical thinking skills.

Our theme "The Second Hour" symbolizes the crucial moment when ideas transform into action—when debate turns into decision, and dialogue becomes diplomacy. It represents the pivotal hour when delegates move from discussion to resolution, embodying the essence of meaningful change.

Our Vision

We envision a world where young leaders are equipped with the skills to address global challenges. ANTRAAL MUN aims to foster a culture of informed debate, cross-cultural understanding, and collaborative problem-solving. Through our conferences, we strive to nurture the diplomats and policymakers of tomorrow.
